Claydee's Studio

Located at the heart of Greece's vibrant music scene, Claydee's Studio has become a leading name in the music industry. Known for creating some of the biggest chart-topping hits, the studio has been a launchpad for collaborations that have resonated globally. The production quality and visionary output have attracted top-tier artists from across the world, eager to work within the studio's creative hub.

One of the studio's most notable hits, "Loquita," was a summer anthem that brought together Claydee and **Eleni Foureira**, one of Greece’s biggest pop stars. The song's infectious rhythm and danceable beats became a staple on airplay charts across Europe. Additionally, Claydee has been instrumental in shaping the sound of **Bang La Decks**, the masked duo known for their high-energy tracks, blending EDM and ethnic sounds.

The studio also contributed to the **Fast & Furious 7** soundtrack with **Sevyn Streeter's** alternate version of "How Bad Do You Want It," which featured a rework of **Bang La Decks’** global hit "Utopia." This song exemplified Claydee’s ability to craft music that works across multiple platforms, including Hollywood blockbusters.

Sevyn Streeter - How Bad Do You Want It (Fast & Furious 7 soundtrack)

Bang La Decks' track "Utopia" was not only a chart success but also featured on **FIFA 15**, gaining worldwide recognition in the gaming industry. This achievement further solidified Claydee’s studio as a breeding ground for globally impactful music.

Another major success from the studio was the viral track "Later Bitches" by **The Prince Karma**. The quirky, infectious track took Europe by storm, becoming a party anthem in clubs and festivals worldwide. The Prince Karma's distinctive sound was shaped and enhanced at Claydee’s Studio, setting a new standard for European electronic music.

With these achievements, Claydee’s Studio has positioned itself at the forefront of European music production, boasting an impressive portfolio that spans across pop, electronic, and soundtrack work. From radio hits to film soundtracks, the studio is committed to pushing the boundaries of music creation, with many more hits to come.
